Online Process Benefits

Paperless Check-in

The Visitor Management System allows guests to check in digitally, removing the need for time-consuming paperwork. Visitor tracking software also gives your visitors a professional first impression.
Notifications

When guests are there, the Visitor Management System alerts the host, so you don't have to waste time looking for them. As soon as the visitor checks in, an instant alert notification is provided to the host, saving time.
Send Invites

Send out invitations to your visitors in advance of their visit. The venue and host information are included in the invitation. When a guest checks in, all he has to do is select the invitee option on the Visitor Management app.
